My Journey: No Sugar-Coating

I'm not your typical success story. I didn't have venture capital, Silicon Valley connections, or even US citizenship when I started.

The Reality

Based in China, minimal resources, trying to crack the US market as a complete outsider. Every "simple" step everyone talks about? Not simple when you're foreign.

LLC formation, tax compliance, understanding US consumer behavior, navigating TikTok's algorithm - I had to figure it all out the hard way.

The Breakthrough

But here's what I discovered: Being an outsider was actually my advantage. I had to be more creative, more resourceful, more data-driven than everyone else.

I couldn't rely on assumptions or "common knowledge" - I had to test everything. That obsession with testing and optimization is what eventually led to breakthrough results in Southeast Asia, and now the US market.

Why I Started Teaching

After scaling my own operations, I realized something: There are thousands of foreign founders facing the exact same struggles I faced.

The information exists, but it's scattered, often assumes you're American, and rarely addresses the unique challenges we face. I started documenting my journey not just for accountability, but because I wished someone had done this for me when I started.

Now, I help other non-US founders navigate this path with proven systems, real data, and strategies that actually work when you don't have traditional advantages.
